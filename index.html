Our Mission

Welcome to "ICHHE PURON"!

We believe in the power of happiness and the profound joy that festivals like Durga Puja bring to our lives. Our charity fund is dedicated to spreading happiness and creating unforgettable moments during this auspicious celebration of Goddess Durga's triumph over evil.

At "ICHEE PURON," our mission is to enhance the joy of individuals and communities during the festive season. We understand that Durga Puja is a time for unity, togetherness, and celebration. It's a moment when families and friends come together to rejoice, and we want to make this experience even more memorable for those who may be facing challenges or hardships.

Our initiatives are designed to uplift spirits, foster community bonds, and create a sense of belonging during this significant festival. We organize events and activities that bring smiles to people's faces, spread love and warmth, and amplify the festive cheer. From community feasts to cultural performances, from distributing essentials to the less fortunate to organizing special events for children and the elderly, we strive to touch every heart and make this Durga Puja a truly magical and inclusive celebration.

Together, let's amplify the happiness, and make Durga Puja a time of unparalleled joy and togetherness for everyone.
